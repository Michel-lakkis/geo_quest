About GeoQuest

GeoQuest is an exciting, interactive geography game designed to challenge and entertain users with a series of fun, educational challenges. Whether you’re a geography enthusiast or just looking to learn more about the world, GeoQuest offers an engaging way to test your knowledge of countries, capitals, and world geography.

With dynamic features like interactive maps, random country selection, and real-time feedback on your answers, GeoQuest provides an immersive learning experience. Our aim is to make learning about the world both enjoyable and informative.

Explore the world through different modes, each designed to help you expand your knowledge of geography. The game will continue to evolve with new challenges and updates, helping you stay on your toes as you journey through the map!
